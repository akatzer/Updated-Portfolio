About Me

I was born in Cincinnati, Ohio and after a few moves in my early years my family and I landed in Olathe, KS. I was in first grade at the time and we have been in the Kansas City area ever since.

I attended high school at Olathe South, graduating in 2005 and began my college career at Johnson County Community College. I attended school there for two years while working full time. After my time at JCCC was up it was off to Kansas State where I graduated in December 2009 with a degree in Business Administration with an emphasis in Human Resources.

I moved back to Olathe after graduation and have worked for AMC Theatres, Farmers Insurance, and my current employer, Swagelok Kansas City, where I have now worked for over 7 years.

My wife Kristen and I were married on September 14th, 2013 (we were highschool sweethearts). Our first child Hunter was born on September 24th, 2016 and baby Katzer #2 is due on November 29th of this year! I also have a dog named Dexter and he is a Pug/Beagle mix.
